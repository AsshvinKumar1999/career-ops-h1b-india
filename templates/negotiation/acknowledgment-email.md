# Acknowledgment Email Template

## Purpose
Buys time to review offer and prepare negotiation strategy.

## Subject
Re: {{company_name}} Offer — {{role_title}}

## Template

Hi {{recruiter_name}},

Thank you so much for the offer for the {{role_title}} position at {{company_name}}. I'm genuinely excited about the opportunity to join the team and contribute to [specific project or goal].

I'm currently reviewing the details of the offer and would like to take a couple of business days to carefully consider everything. Is that workable for you?

I'll follow up with my decision by {{date + 2-3 business days}}.

Thank you again for this opportunity. I look forward to hearing from you.

Best regards,
{{your_name}}
```

## Variables

| Variable | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| `{{company_name}}` | Company name |
| `{{role_title}}` | Job title |
| `{{recruiter_name}}` | Recruiter's name |
| `{{date}}` | Today's date |
