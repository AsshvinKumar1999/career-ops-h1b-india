# Counter Offer Email Template

## Purpose
Submits a formal counter offer with supporting rationale.

## Subject
Re: {{company_name}} Offer — {{role_title}}

## Template

Hi {{recruiter_name}},

Thank you for the offer for the {{role_title}} position. I'm genuinely enthusiastic about the opportunity to join {{company_name}}.

After careful consideration of the offer and current market conditions for this role, I'd like to propose a counter offer of {{counter_salary}} total compensation. This reflects [market data / my experience / the scope of the role].

I'm also happy to discuss alternative compensation structures, including:
- {{benefit_1}} (e.g., signing bonus)
- {{benefit_2}} (e.g., additional equity)
- {{benefit_3}} (e.g., flexible PTO policy)

Based on my research and experience, I believe this adjustment is fair and appropriate for the value I'll bring to the team. I'd welcome the opportunity to discuss this further.

Thank you for your understanding, and I look forward to our conversation.

Best regards,
{{your_name}}
```

## Variables

| Variable | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| `{{counter_salary}}` | Your counter offer amount |
| `{{benefit_1}}` | First alternative benefit |
