# Phone Negotiation Talking Points

## Before the Call

- [ ] Know your walk-away number (minimum)
- [ ] Know your target number
- [ ] Know your opening number
- [ ] Research comparable salaries
- [ ] Prepare 2-3 specific achievements
- [ ] Have benefits list ready as alternatives

## Opening

Start with genuine enthusiasm:
> "Hi [Name], thank you so much for the offer. I'm really excited about [Company] and the [Role] position."

Brief pause — let them respond.

## Making Your Ask

Be direct and confident:
> "Based on my research and experience, I was hoping we could discuss a compensation package of [Opening Number]."

## Objection Handling

### "That's our best offer"
> "I appreciate that. Based on my research, roles similar to this one typically range [X-Y]. Is there flexibility on [benefit]?"

### "We have budget constraints"
> "I understand. Would it be possible to address part of the gap through [signing bonus / equity / additional PTO]?"

## Closing

Always end positively:
> "Thank you so much for your time. I look forward to hearing back from you."
